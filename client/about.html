About Farm2Table

Farm2Table is a modern ecommerce platform designed to connect farmers directly with consumers. Our mission is to empower local producers, promote fresh and healthy food, and simplify the farm-to-table experience.

Our Features

  • Direct farmer-to-consumer sales
  • Product grid with local images
  • Order tracking and checkout
  • Farmer dashboard and analytics
  • Community chat and reviews
  • Multi-language and currency support
